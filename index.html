About Dazzle Donuts

A locally owned donut store made to treat the South West. We have recently opened to the public and are proud and eager to sell our donuts baked daily in store. Our store is located in the heart of Treendale, Western Australia for customer convenience. Our goal is to give customers an easily accessible donut service throughout Australia, to seamlessly provide donuts for everyone to enjoy at any time of the day.

Donut Services

We offer donuts hand crafted with consideration for every individual treat. A fun family experience, try our broad variety of delicate sugary rings.
We have a wide selection of treats available 8:30am-5:00pm weekdays and 7:00am-6:00pm weekends.

El Homer

Dazzle Donut's signature treat, named after the infamous Simpsons donut. A huge fried donut coated with delicious strawberry glaze and rainbow sprinkles.

Jelly Donut

A classic fan favourite, packed with fruit jam and fresh cream. Served either warm or cold depending on personal preference.

Glazed Donut

The original donut, simply frosted in a glazed icing. Flavours include coffee, chocolate, vanilla or maple.
